Emphasis and Contrast

petco

Visit petco.com!

The website for petco illistrates emphasis and contrast by the foreground, background, and text color. The blocks of color that are dark in front of the white background make it pop. The same is true for the text color that is white ontop of the dark blocks. Your eyes are immediately drawn to th words.

Color

Vans

Visit Vans.com!

The website for Vans illistrates an excellent use of color in the first box on the screen. When the website is in full view it's yellow and blue which are complimentary colors. When in mobile view the blue part of the box goes away but you are still left with the blue shoes ontop of the yellow background so it still pops and immediatelycatches your eye.

Repetition

Zillow

Visit Zillow.com!

The website for Zillow has immediate Repetition. Although it's all not seen in the screenshot above, there are 3 boxes that say "Buy a Home," "Rent a Home," and "Sell a Home" all in the same style one after another. It is very visually pleasing becasue of this repetition.
